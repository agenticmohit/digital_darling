Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Who we are

DigitalDarling is a product of Yuvneer ProCommerce LLP, a company registered in India. We operate the website and service available at digitaldarling.app ("Service"). This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how it is protected.

Questions? Reach us at hello@digitaldarling.app

Data we collect

Account data

Your email address, collected when you sign up or use magic link login. Used to identify your account and send transactional emails only.

Chat content

Text you paste or screenshots you upload for analysis. This content is sent to OpenAI's API for processing and stored in your personal history. We do not read, share, or use your conversations for training.

Usage data

A daily read counter (resets at midnight) to enforce plan limits. No browsing behaviour, device fingerprinting, or analytics tracking.

Payment data

Payments are processed by Razorpay. We receive your email and plan status — your card number or UPI details never touch our servers.

Third-party services

OpenAI Chat analysis & vision
Supabase Authentication & database
Razorpay Payment processing

Each provider operates under their own privacy policy. We select providers who adhere to strong data protection standards.

Data retention & deletion

Your account and history are retained as long as your account is active. To delete your account and all associated data, email us at hello@digitaldarling.app and we will process it within 7 business days.

Your rights

Under Indian law (IT Act, 2000) and applicable data protection regulations, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. Contact us at hello@digitaldarling.app to exercise any of these rights.

Security

Authentication tokens are stored in secure, HTTP-only cookies. Passwords are never stored — we use Supabase Auth which handles hashing and security. All data is transmitted over HTTPS.
